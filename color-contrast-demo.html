WCAG AA Color Contrast Compliance

\n

All colors meet WCAG AA standards with minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for normal text and 3:1 for large text

\n\n \n
\n

WCAG AA Requirements

\n
    \n
  • Normal text: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio
    • \n
  • Large text (18pt+ or 14pt+ bold): Minimum 3:1 contrast ratio
    • \n
  • Non-text elements: Minimum 3:1 contrast ratio for UI components
    • \n
  • Focus indicators: Minimum 3:1 contrast ratio against adjacent colors
    • \n
